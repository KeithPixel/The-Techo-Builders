About The Techno-Builders

The Techno-Builders (TTB), the official student publication of the University of Southern Mindanao-Kidapawan City Campus, is an autonomous, non-organizational, or non-academic guild composed of student writers and artists. Training, developing, and exposing journalists in various fields such as Editorial, News, Feature and Literary Writing, Cartooning and Visual Arts, Photography, Videography, Graphic Designing, and Layouting. TTB, being directly under the administration, is also assessed for International Organization for Standardization (ISO) certifications and recertifications.

Constitution and Bylaws

Our organization is guided by a constitution that ensures fairness, transparency, and a commitment to our mission. It outlines our structure, membership, and operational procedures.

Dr. Rowena Vasquez-Sosas

Associate Professor V

Dr. Rowena Vasquez-Sosas is a lecturer of research and linguistics, the Director of Quality Assurance, the management representative, and the Adviser of Student Publication of the University of Southern Mindanao, Kidapawan City Campus, Philippines.

She is a module writer with seven copyright certificates, a well-cited language researcher with 5 h index; an awardee of Gawad Lingkod Bayan by the University of Southern Mindanao on September 27, 2024; a Best Paper and Best Oral Presenter at the 4th International Convention Cum TESOL Conference on July 28-31, 2022 in Bangkok, Thailand; a national and international research presenter, a reviewer and member of the External Advisory Board of USLS Journals of the University of St. La Salle, Bacolod, the Lasallianum Ars et Scientia, and a language lecturer-reviewer for Professional Regulation Commission on Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers.

Most importantly, she is a proud member of the Iglesia Ni Cristo (Church of Christ) and is performing a holy duty in finance.
